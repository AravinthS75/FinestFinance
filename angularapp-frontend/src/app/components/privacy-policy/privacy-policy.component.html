Finest Finance ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information...

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, PAN, Aadhaar, income details
  • Financial Information: Bank statements, credit history, transaction records
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, usage patterns
  • Third-party Data: Credit bureau reports, KYC verification data

Your information is used for:

  • Loan eligibility assessment
  • Identity verification (KYC)
  • Credit risk analysis
  • Service improvement
  • Regulatory compliance

We may share information with:

  • Credit bureaus (CIBIL, Experian, Equifax)
  • Banking partners and payment gateways
  • Legal and regulatory authorities
  • Fraud prevention agencies
  • Third-party service providers

We implement security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access control
  • Secure data storage
  • Two-factor authentication

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)
  • Obtain data portability

We use cookies to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Remember preferences
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Improve service delivery

Manage cookies through browser settings or our consent manager.

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via:

  • Email to registered users
  • Website banner notifications
  • Updated effective date
